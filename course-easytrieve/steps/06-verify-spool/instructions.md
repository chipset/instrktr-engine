# Jobs, Spool, and Completion Codes

After submission, Zowe Explorer shows a notification with the **Job ID**. You follow that into the **Jobs** view, expand the job, and open DDs such as **SYSPRINT** to read the Easytrieve listing.

A successful run typically shows a completion code of **`0000`** in the job status (site messages vary, but `0000` is the common “good” indicator called out in the workflow).
