# Upload and submit

## Extension settings

- `eztdsn` → `MYUSER.DEV.EZT.SRC`
- `jcldsn` → `MYUSER.DEV.EZT.JCL`

## Actions

1. Upload from the Easytrieve source file: **Upload EZT to Mainframe**
2. Submit the job: right-click the JCL member in **Zowe Explorer** and choose **Submit Job**, or right-click the JCL file and use **Submit Job** from Easytrieve Language Support.
